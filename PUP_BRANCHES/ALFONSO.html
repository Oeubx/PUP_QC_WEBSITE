Polytechnic University of the Philippines
Alfonso Branch

Branch Location:

PUP-Alfonso Campus is an extension of Maragondon Branch in Cavite, an emerging premier university in the Municipality of Alfonso, Cavite.

Courses Offered:

Bachelor of Science in Accountancy

Bachelor in Secondary Education major in English

Bachelor in Secondary Education major in Mathematics

Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering

Other Details:

With its strategic location, the campus aims to generously serve the people of Alfonso and nearby towns.

Tagged as the youngest PUP campus, this newly established institution has produced numerous professionals in various fields and vows to continuously serve the indigent and most deserving students across Alfonso, Cavite.
