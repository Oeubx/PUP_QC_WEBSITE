Other Details:

The Polytechnic University of the Philippines San Pedro Campus offers the scholastic hopefuls an opportunity to have transformative lives through the extraordinary power of education.

PUP San Pedro fully commits itself to the community, motivated to realize the dream of every youth in the city, with the campus purposeful mission.

Polytechnic University of the Philippines has been in San Pedro City, Laguna for two decades, in pursuit of its quest in bringing life-changing educational experiences regardless of socio-cultural standings. Indeed, education serves as an equalizer to achieve social mobility and become active and productive members of society.

PUP San Pedro, despite daunting odds, has managed to survive vigorously, courageously, and even heroically to fulfill in avowed mission as enshrined in the PUP Philosophy and University's mission, vision, and guiding core values.

Evidently, PUP San Pedro Campus has served and is continuously serving its mission to quality educational services, transcending into one of PUP's premiere campuses in the South.
