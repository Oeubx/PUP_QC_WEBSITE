Other Details:

Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Sta. Rosa (PUP-Sta. Rosa) and is one of the two extension campuses of the university system in Laguna, Philippines.

It is currently located at Tiongco Subdivision, Brgy. Tagapo, Santa Rosa City, Laguna, and was established in 2003.

The campus is one of the four (4) PUP campuses in the province of Laguna. The Sta. Rosa campus delivers various academic programs In the fields of Business, Teacher Education, Engineering, Computer Studies, and Behavioral Sciences.
