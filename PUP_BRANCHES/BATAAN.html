Other Details:

The Polytechnic University of the Philippines Mariveles, Bataan Branch is the only state university located at the heart of the Freeport Area of Bataan which provides industry driven and globally competitive tertiary education in the province.

It commits itself in empowering its stakeholders through excellent service delivery through inclusive instruction, relevant research, and empowering extension.

The Bachelor in Elementary Education, a program funded by the local government of Bataan, produces competitive and compassionate Licensed Professional Teachers in both private and public schools in the locality.

The campus also boasts graduates of the Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering and Bachelor of Science in Accountancy make their way in improving the operations and processes in the various locators in the Freeport involved in manufacturing, logistics, energy, electronics, infrastructure, tourism, shipbuilding, and business process outsourcing
