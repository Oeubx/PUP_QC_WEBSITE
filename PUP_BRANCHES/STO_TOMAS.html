Polytechnic University of the Philippines
Sto. Tomas Branch

Branch Location:

This branch is located at A. Bonifacio St. Poblacion II, Sto. Tomas, Batangas (south of Metro Manila).

Courses Offered :

Bachelor in Elementary Education

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Human Resource Management

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Marketing Management

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Bachelor of Science in Office Administration

Bachelor of Secondary Education major in English

Other Details:

The PUP Sto. Tomas Branch, consistent with the national development thrust and mission of the University, envisions itself to be a center of excellence in instruction, research and extension in the fields of education, business, accountancy, social science, engineering and information technology.

We achieve this through affordable and quality education aimed at developing professionals imbued with nationalistic and democratic virtues and committed to the enhancement of a dignified, progressive and productive life.

This branch commits itself in providing quality education to the underprivileged but deserving students and serving the marginalized members of the community through extension and community services.
