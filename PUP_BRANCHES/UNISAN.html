Other Details:

The Polytechnic University of the Philippines-Unisan Branch is deeply committed to provide quality and free education to improve the lives of the people in Bondoc Peninsula- the third district in the province of Quezon.

Our mission is to keep the teaching and learning environment of the campus abreast with national and international standards and demands.

The academic programs are meticulously and strategically designed to hone the Indispensable knowledge, skills and competencies.

These include Bachelor of Elementary Education, Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship, Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, Diploma in Office Management Technology- Major In Legal Transcription and Diploma in Information and Communication Technology. Through these programs, we have produced graduates who are well rounded and equipped for the professional world.

Thus, we have our slogan, "PUP Unisan Branch: Educating Minds, Elevating Lives"
