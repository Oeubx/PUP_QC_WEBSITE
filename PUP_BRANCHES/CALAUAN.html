PUP Calauan Campus envisions itself to be a key player in the locality of Calauan, Laguna; committed to making quality education and fundamental training accessible to produce professionally competent, service-oriented, principled, and productive graduates who are future leaders in their chosen fields of endeavor.

The Bachelor in Business Technology and Livelihood Education, Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship, and Bachelor of Science in Information Technology echo the general goal of developing individuals who are academically proficient and professionally excellent.
