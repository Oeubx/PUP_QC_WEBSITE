Polytechnic University of the Philippines
Cabiao Branch

Branch Location:

Established in 1996 as a Pamantasang Bayan of the PUP Open University. In 2009, it became a campus and is located at San Roque, Cabiao, Nueva Ecija.

Courses Offered:

Bachelor in Elementary Education

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Marketing Management

Other Details:

From a Pamantasang Bayan that offers PUP Open University Tech-Voc programs from 1996 to 2009, the PUP Cabiao Campus has flourished into a full-fledge educational institution and currently offers Bachelor in Elementary Education and Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Marketing Management programs.
